## Important Notes

1. **Peak Season Pricing:** Late June through mid-July is peak travel season in the Northern Hemisphere. Prices are typically higher, and advance booking is recommended.

2. **Price Variability:** All prices listed are approximate estimates based on recent data. Actual fares fluctuate based on:
   - Booking date
   - Demand levels
   - Fare class selected
   - Promotional offers
   - Seasonal adjustments

3. **New Routes:** China Airlines began direct Taipei-Seattle flights on July 14, 2024, which may affect pricing and availability toward the end of the target period.

4. **Verification Recommended:** Always verify current flight schedules, availability, and pricing directly with:
   - Airline websites
   - Travel aggregators (Skyscanner, Google Flights, Expedia, etc.)
   - Official airport information

5. **Alternative Cities:** Other Chinese tier-1 cities like Guangzhou and Shenzhen generally do not have consistent direct flights to Seattle during this period. Connecting flights via partner airlines (Delta, United, and their SkyTeam/Star Alliance/Oneworld partners) are commonly available.
