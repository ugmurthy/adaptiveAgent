In the quiet of the morning light,
Where shadows dance with golden hue,
The world awakes from peaceful night,
And dreams begin to fade from view.

The birds sing songs of hope and grace,
As dewdrops gleam on leaves so green,
Time moves at its own gentle pace,
The most beautiful scene ever seen.

May joy find you in every day,
And peace reside within your heart,
Let love be what you choose to say,
Before each new beginning starts.
