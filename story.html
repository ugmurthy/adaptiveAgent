Meet Puff!

🦖

Once upon a time, in a land with green hills and blue sky, there lived a tiny dragon who didn't breathe fire. He burped bubbles!

The Problem

☁️

Every other dragon breathed hot fire in caves. But Puff wanted to play with clouds like a fluffy bunny!

The Magic Discovery

🌈

Puff found a glowing blue jewel that hummed with magic. When Puff hugged it, it became his magic hug!

Wonderful Adventures

⛰️

Puff flew higher than eagles! He gave gentle hugs to sheep and taught them to be brave and kind to everyone!

Best Friends Forever

🐑

Little sheep named Barnaby became Puff's best friend. Together they explored shiny caves and found many beautiful treasures!

A Magical Celebration

🎉

All the villagers came to see Puff's special hug. They celebrated with rainbow confetti and delicious berry pies!

The Happy Ending

🌟

Puff showed everyone that being different is a special gift. Love and kindness are the most powerful magic of all!
